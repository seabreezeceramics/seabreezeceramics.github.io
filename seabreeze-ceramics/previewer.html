Seabreeze Ceramics

Seabreeze Ceramics is a small, family run business specialising in personalised ceramic gifts and unique paw print tiles of your pets.

Seabreeze ceramics is a business created by Gina Langridge. Gina gained a BA in Glassblowing and Ceramics from the Buckinghamshire New University and a foundation Diploma in Ceramics and Fine art from Wimbledon College of the Arts.

Gina creates these beautiful handmade tiles of pawprint impressions using her favourite clays, glazes, colours and techniques to give the best finish to all her products.

The process is simple!

  • We post you out a kit in a prepaid return postage box.
  • You get your animals pawprint using the tile.
  • Send us back the kit in the prepaid envelope.
  • We'll make a beautiful tile of your dog/cat/rabbit/bird's pawprint!

The kit contains a special art dough to get the imprint of your animals paw, instructions on how to take the imprint and an order form for you to choose the colours and text for your tile.
